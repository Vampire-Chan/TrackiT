About TrackIT

Who we are

Founder & Teammates

At TrackIT, our mission is to revolutionize public transportation and personal safety through innovative tracking solutions. Our platform offers real-time tracking of government buses and public transport, while also enabling individuals to share their location with family members for peace of mind. Our system combines a user-friendly frontend, robust backend logic, and a secure database to deliver a seamless experience. With intuitive design, TrackIT ensures users can easily access real-time bus locations, share their whereabouts, and stay connected. Whether you're commuting or tracking loved ones, TrackIT provides the tools you need for safe and efficient travel.
